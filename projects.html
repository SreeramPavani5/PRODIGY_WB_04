Tic-Tac game

A game in which two players take turns in drawing either an ` O' or an ` X' in one square of a grid consisting of nine squares.You are X , your friend (or the computer in this case) is O . Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares. The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner
